Awe50me Weekend

Awe50me Weekend 2019

One of the milestone events of On the Streets of Chennai’s (OTS), the most challenging yet enjoyable one, was ‘Awe50me Weekend’. To celebrate our 50th anniversary and add fervour to Chennai’s cultural month of December and its famed music season, our members organized themselves into bands of twos or threes or quartets and performed 50 across in multiple venues on 2 days (14 and 15 December 2019).

Hotspots of the city like Pondy Bazaar, Nageshwar Rao Park and Besant Nagar beach were ablaze with music as OTS members serenaded people from classic, evergreen covers by AR Rahman and Illayaraja, modern rock and pop, to multilingual mashups.

As stated earlier, our aim was to spread smiles and positivity through music on the streets and encourage strangers to break into busking — and we did just that! It gave us sheer joy to see Chennai locals, millennial shoppers and even tourists who were in the city for that weekend stop by and get inspired us trying to revive the nascent street music spirit.

We felt a deep rush of accomplishment when people approached us, started singing with us and followed us all the way for all our shows on those 2 days. We rounded off our evening with the 50th show at pH Cafe, Egmore, which also doubles up as OTS’ jamming studio, and the icing on the cake was witnessing all of our audience, who followed our music and schedule for those 2 days, at pH. We felt a deep sense of gratitude for them having stuck with us till the very end.

As hectic as the event was, our preparations were even more frazzled. It was a busy month of bustling around with flyers and posters, meeting the press, following up with our bands on their rehearsals and availing permissions from the necessary authorities. We are grateful for every helping hand we received, such as city police commissioner, metrorail officials, Greater Chennai Corporation and Rotary Club.

In the end, we were thrilled to end the year with music, just as we started, and we sure made memories that will last a lifetime.
