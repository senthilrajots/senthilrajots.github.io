‘On the Streets of Chennai’ is a motley, vibrant community of musicians, seeking to give back and improve the busking culture of Chennai by spreading smiles and positivity through music. We are an organic, ever-loving culture adventure. We have a fine blend of professional and amateur singers, from a range of musical backgrounds and influences, contributing to the community’s strength and diversity. As a bunch of buskers, our community enjoys the idea of breaking into spontaneous music, reducing barriers across strangers and bridging the gap between the audience and the artist. In the course of a year, it has grown exponentially, membership and otherwise, and OTS is now a close-knit family. We warmly welcome newcomers, be it singers, instrumentalists, percussionists, guitarists or just anyone with an ear for good music, and find joy in working, ideating, and collaborating with them.


What started as a small group of five to seven people in January 2019, now houses almost 150 members and 2000 followers. Members of On The Streets have been performing originals, covers and remixes, spanning genres, in public spaces since its inception and spreading smiles along the way. Some of the prestigious venues include Railway Stations, Metro stations, Beaches, Parks, Pondy Bazaar and various malls dotted across the city.


'On The Streets' does not accept monetary benefits from members or venue partners. Ensuring pulling off hassle-free and hindrance-free gigs to the public and sorting out permissions from the required authorities in advance is key. We also adhere to a zero-tolerance policy to negativity, within and outside the community. The community works with the motto of fairness towards every member. The members strongly believe that music is a democratic space that should be accessible and available for all and OTS strives to adhere to the same.

Our Guiding values

INSPIRE | Inspire people to be positive

PRIDE | Take pride in what we do

ONLY POSITIVITY | Fairness to everyone

GROWTH | Get better every single day


Why guiding values ?

To operate independently and make decisions on our own in difficult situations.

INSPIRE

Each member of OTS is a leader and will constantly inspire others for great things. E.g. Good vibes, Smiles, Good singing, Style, RAOK etc.,

We will be vulnerable to be infected by good things.

We will avoid negativity like poison.

PRIDE

We will take pride in doing Kick-ass stuffs.

Even when things go wrong, we will take pride in the learning that makes us improve.

 

 

POSITIVITY

Our tolerance for negativity, politics will be very low.

We will be fair to ourselves and others at every situation.

No favors or obligations expected here- please have something to take away from OTS.

We strictly do not accept money from our audience, venue partners or members.

We contribute to the society as proud givers.

We do not fight for publicity – Great performers can’t be ignored by media. Focus is to get great at our skills.

We thank very often as a habit and never complain.

If you have a problem, lets fix it. We don’t crib here as leaders and doers.

GROWTH

We will constantly show growth: As a leader, in music, personality, communication, managing things and lifestyle.

Consistency is the key.

Feedback is the crux. Being open to taking feedback and working on them is essential for the performers.

Positive feedback will encourage us to continue the good job.

'Can be better' feedback will make us better.

We respect our audience and we owe them the best.

We respect our performers and co-members, they get better through honest feedback and practice.
